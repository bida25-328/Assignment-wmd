Park 1 — Gaborone

Directions

Plot 5432, Lobatse Road
Gaborone, Botswana

From the CBD: take the A1 south toward Lobatse, exit at the Game City interchange and follow the brown heritage signs for 3 km.

Operating Hours

  • Mon – Fri: 08:00 – 17:00
  • Saturday: 08:00 – 18:00
  • Sunday: 09:00 – 15:00
  • Public Holidays: 09:00 – 14:00

Tickets

  • Adults: P80
  • Children (under 12): P40
  • Students (with ID): P55
  • Family pack (2+2): P220

Park 2 — Francistown

Directions

Stand 881, Blue Jacket Street
Francistown, Botswana

From the town centre: head north on Blue Jacket Street past Spar Mall. The sanctuary entrance is on your left, clearly signposted.

Operating Hours

  • Mon – Fri: 08:00 – 17:00
  • Saturday: 08:00 – 17:00
  • Sunday: Closed
  • Public Holidays: 10:00 – 14:00

Tickets

  • Adults: P75
  • Children (under 12): P35
  • Students (with ID): P50
  • Family pack (2+2): P200
